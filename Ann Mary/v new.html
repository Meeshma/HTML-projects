Rom&nd 







 
Korean Beauty Brand: Rom&nd is a popular Korean beauty brand known for its trendy and innovative makeup products.

Founder: The brand was founded by Saerom Min, a well-known beauty influencer and makeup artist in South Korea.

Vibrant Colors: Rom&nd is famous for its vibrant and highly pigmented lip products, including the Zero Velvet Tint and Juicy Lasting Tint.

Unique Packaging: The brand's products are known for their sleek and stylish packaging, which appeals to a wide range of customers.

Versatile Products: Rom&nd offers a variety of makeup products, including lip tints, eyeshadows, blushes, and foundations, catering to different makeup preferences and styles.

Global Popularity: Rom&nd has gained a significant following not only in South Korea but also internationally, thanks to its high-quality products and effective marketing strategies.
