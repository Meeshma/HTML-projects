IORN-MAN







Iron Man, also known as Tony Stark, is a fictional superhero in the Marvel Comics universe created by writer Stan Lee, along with scripter Larry Lieber and artists Don Heck and Jack Kirby. He made his first appearance in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963. Tony Stark is a wealthy industrialist and genius inventor who dons a high-tech suit of armor to become Iron Man, using his advanced technology to fight crime and protect the world. The character is known for his intelligence, charisma, and complex personality, balancing his playboy lifestyle with his responsibilities as a superhero. Iron Man has become a central figure in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, portrayed by actor Robert Downey Jr., and is celebrated for his significant contributions to the Avengers and the larger Marvel storyline.
