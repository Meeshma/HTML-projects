HULK







The Hulk, also known as Bruce Banner, is a fictional superhero in the Marvel Comics universe created by writer Stan Lee and artist Jack Kirby. He made his first appearance in The Incredible Hulk #1 in 1962. Bruce Banner is a brilliant scientist who, after being exposed to gamma radiation, transforms into the powerful and green-skinned Hulk whenever he becomes angry or stressed. The Hulk possesses immense strength, durability, and regenerative capabilities, making him one of the most formidable beings in the Marvel Universe. Despite his destructive potential, the Hulk often uses his abilities to protect humanity and fight against various threats.
