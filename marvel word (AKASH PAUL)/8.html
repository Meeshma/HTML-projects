CAPTAIN AMERICA







Captain America, also known as Steve Rogers, is a fictional superhero in the Marvel Comics universe created by writer Joe Simon and artist Jack Kirby. He first appeared in Captain America Comics #1 in 1941, during World War II, and quickly became an iconic patriotic symbol. Steve Rogers was transformed into the super-soldier Captain America after being injected with the Super-Soldier Serum, which granted him enhanced strength, agility, and endurance. Wielding his iconic shield, Captain America is known for his unwavering bravery, leadership, and sense of justice. He has been a central figure in the Avengers and has been portrayed by actor Chris Evans in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, further cementing his legacy as a beloved hero.
