DOCTOR STRANGE







Doctor Strange, also known as Dr. Stephen Strange, is a fictional superhero in the Marvel Comics universe created by writer Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko. He made his first appearance in Strange Tales #110 in 1963. Originally a brilliant but arrogant neurosurgeon, Dr. Strange's life changed forever after a car accident damaged his hands, rendering him unable to perform surgery. Desperate for a cure, he sought out the Ancient One and eventually became the Sorcerer Supreme, mastering the mystical arts to protect the Earth from supernatural threats. With his powerful artifacts, such as the Eye of Agamotto and Cloak of Levitation, Doctor Strange is known for his wisdom, magical prowess, and dedication to safeguarding the multiverse.
