ANT-MAN







Ant-Man, also known as Scott Lang, is a fictional superhero in the Marvel Comics universe. Created by writer David Michelinie and artist John Byrne, he first appeared in The Avengers #181 in 1979. As Ant-Man, Scott Lang has the ability to shrink to the size of an ant while retaining his human strength, thanks to the Pym Particles developed by the original Ant-Man, Hank Pym. Scott Lang is known for his cleverness, bravery, and his roles in both solo adventures and as a member of the Avengers. His character has been brought to life in the Marvel Cinematic Universe, where he is portrayed by actor Paul Rudd.
