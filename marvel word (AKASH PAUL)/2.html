SPIDERMAN







Spider-Man, also known as Peter Parker, is a beloved fictional superhero created by writer Stan Lee and writer-artist Steve Ditko. He first appeared in Amazing Fantasy #15 in 1962, published by Marvel Comics. Spider-Man is known for his incredible agility, web-slinging abilities, and "spidey-sense" that alerts him to danger. By day, Peter Parker is a mild-mannered high school student and photographer, but by night he fights crime as Spider-Man, driven by the mantra "With great power comes great responsibility." The character's enduring popularity has led to numerous adaptations in film, television, and video games.
