Popular Calisthenics Exercises

Pull-up

One of the best upper body exercises to strengthen the back and biceps.

Push-up

Great for chest, shoulders, and triceps. Perfect for beginners to advanced levels.

Muscle-up

Advanced movement combining a pull-up and dip, building incredible upper body strength.

Tips for Success in Street Workout

Start Slow

Begin with the basics like push-ups and squats, and progressively challenge yourself with advanced movements.

Form is Key

Proper form helps prevent injury and ensures you're maximizing the benefits of each exercise.
